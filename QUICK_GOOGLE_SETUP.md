## ‚ö° Quick Steps to Get Indexed on Google

### Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console (5 minutes)

1. **Go to**: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Click**: "Add Property"
3. **Enter**: `graphclic.com` (or `https://graphclic.com`)
4. **Choose verification method**:
   - **Easiest**: HTML tag verification
   - Copy the verification code Google gives you
   - Tell me the code and I'll add it to your site
5. **After verification**: Submit your sitemap: `https://graphclic.com/sitemap.xml`

### Step 2: Request Indexing (2 minutes)

1. In Google Search Console, go to **"URL Inspection"**
2. Enter: `https://graphclic.com`
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**
4. Repeat for:
   - `https://graphclic.com/services`
   - `https://graphclic.com/our-work`
   - `https://graphclic.com/about`
   - `https://graphclic.com/contact`

### Step 3: Wait (Patience Required!)

- **1-3 days**: Google starts crawling
- **1-2 weeks**: Site appears in search results
- **2-4 weeks**: Full indexing complete
